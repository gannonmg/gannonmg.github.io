Recommended Albums

Album: Sour Soul
Artist: BADBADNOTGOOD & Ghostface Killah
Genre: Jazz-infused hip-hop
Why you should listen: Sour Soul is the combination of music group (they are not a jazz group, they insist) BADBADNOTGOOD and legendary rapper and Wu-Tang Clan member Ghostface Killah. BBNG got their start doing hip-hop jazz covers, which caught the eye of Tyler, The Creator. Since then, they've been band-in-residence at Coachella (backing Frank Ocean both weekends of the festival), helped RZA produce the soundtrack to his film The Man with the Iron Fists, and eventually created this album. It should go without saying, but GFK is a founding member of the Wu-Tang Clan, and has released over a dozen solo albums and six more collaboration albums in his career. Together, this collection of artists get back to BBNG roots to create one of the most soulful hip-hop albums I've ever heard. It's an incredible blending of genres and talents, and anyone with a remote interest in hip-hop and jazz owes it to themself to give this album a listen hear what I'm talking about.
If you liked this: Check out BADBADNOTGOOD's IV and Ghostface Killah's 36 Seasons
